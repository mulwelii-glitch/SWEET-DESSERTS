Our Story

Sweet Desserts was born from a simple passion: to create desserts that feel like a warm hug. What started as a tiny home kitchen experiment in 2016 has grown into a beloved local bakery, but our heart remains the same — every treat is made from scratch with real ingredients and genuine love.



Meet the Baker

Carol Ratshilumela, the heart behind Sweet Dessert, grew up surrounded by the aroma of butter and sugar in her grandmother's kitchen. After training in paris and working in top patisseries at a young age, she returned to Cape Town to share her dream: a bakery where quality meets comfort. Every recipe is inspired by family memories and perfected through patience.

“I believe dessert is more than just sugar — it’s an experience that brings people together. That’s why we never compromise on ingredients or care.” — Carol

What We Believe In

  • Real Ingredients: No shortcuts. We use European butter, free-range eggs, and pure vanilla.
  • Made Fresh Daily: Everything is baked in small batches — nothing sits on the shelf for days.
  • Community Love: We support local farmers and give back to neighbourhood initiatives.
  • Inclusive Treats: Gluten-free, nut-free, and vegan options available so everyone can enjoy.


From Our Kitchen to Your Table

Each item on our menu tells a story:

  • Cakes: Layered with care, balanced sweetness, and artistic finishes — perfect for celebrations or just because.
  • Muffins: Jumbo-sized, packed with real fruit or chocolate, and never dry.
  • Scones: Traditional buttermilk recipe, baked until golden, served with our house-made jam.
  • ice cream:Made with Fresh milk, with real fruits
  • Candy & Confections: Hand-picked gourmet sweets and house-made fudge that bring back childhood joy.

We also offer custom cake designs for birthdays, weddings, and special events. Contact us 48 hours in advance to create something unique.

Our Journey

  • 2016: Sweet Dessert opens as a tiny takeaway window in Woodstock.
  • 2017: We move to our current location on Sugar Lane, adding a cosy café space.
  • 2019: Launch of our gluten-free range after customer requests.
  • 2022: Named “Best Bakery” in Cape Town by local food guide.
  • Today: Still family-owned, still baking with the same dedication — and always welcoming new faces.
